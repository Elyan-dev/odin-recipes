Lasagna Recipe

Description

Classic lasagna requires lasagna noodles, meat (ground beef/sausage), pasta sauce, and cheeses (ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan). Essential aromatics and binders include onion, garlic, eggs, and seasonings like Italian seasoning, basil, and parsley. It is typically layered and baked at for 45–90 minutes

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • meat(ground beef/sausage)
  • pasta sauce
  • cheeses(mozzarella, Parmesan)
  • Aromantics and Seasonings(Read description for which ones to use)

Basic preperation steps

  1. Sauce: Brown beef and onion, add garlic, then simmer with pasta sauce and seasonings.
  2. Cheese Mix: Combine ricotta, egg, parsley, and some Parmesan in a bowl.
  3. Assemble: Layer sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella in a 9x13 pan; repeat layers.
  4. Bake: Cover with foil and bake until bubbly.
